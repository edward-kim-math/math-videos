This gives us the numerator of the improper fraction, and the denominator of the improper fraction is just the denominator of the fractional part.

Our numerator is the whole number 10 times the denominator 4, plus the numerator 3. The denominator copies the original denominator 4.
Simplifying the numerator, we have 43 over 4 as our improper fraction.
